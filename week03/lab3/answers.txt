sort1 uses: BUBBLE SORT

How do you know?: It has the best performance with sorted elements. It has the worst performance with reversed elements.

sort2 uses: MERGE SORT

How do you know?: It keeps a good performance for all 3 cases.

sort3 uses: SELECTION SORT

How do you know?: It has the worst performance for the sorted and random cases. Just  surpass the "bubble sort" performance when the lists are reversed.
